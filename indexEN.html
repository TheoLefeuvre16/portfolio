2020-2021

Electric scooter simulator in virtual reality. It is a device allowing to simulate the sensations perceived during the practice of the electric scooter. For that we use two motors which communicate with Unity using an electronic card ESP32. We can thus recreate the sensations of speed, slope and rotation
